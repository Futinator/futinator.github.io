How to get Futinator +

In three simple steps

1. Download Futinator + here:

Latest version: 2.0.1.2. (New feature: log file)

2. Unzip the file

After you have downloaded the file, unzip/extract the file.

(Free) Programs for unzipping:
Windows: https://www.7-zip.org/
Mac: https://theunarchiver.com/

3. Add the file to google chrome

1. Go to Google chrome and click on three dots in the upper right corner.

2. Click on more tools, extensions and enable developer mode in the upper right corner.

3. Click on "Load Unpacked" and select the unziped folder.

4. If the Futinator logo is not visible click on the puzzle icon in the upper right corner (and feel free to pin the app.).

5. Enter your key in the box and press the "Activate" button.
